    • Pothos

    Affectionaly called Devil's Ivy, pothos are a great beginner houseplant.

    Care Guide

    Sunlight
    Pothos thrive in basically any light. From low light
    to bright, direct lighting, pothos are hard to kill.
    TIP: Try placing on a high shelf & watch as the
    vines of the plant grow long in a downward
    cascade.

    Water
    Pothos, like sunlight, can grow with any type
    of water schedule. Mostly, however, they thrive best
    when the first two inches of soil feel dry.

    Fertilizer
    For optimal growth, pothos plants should be fertilized every few months.
    Typically, pothos should be fertilized during growing season (Spring).

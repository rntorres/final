    • ZZ Plant

    ZZ Plant, also known as a Zamioculcas, is one of the easiest plants to care for!

    Care Guide

    Sunlight
    ZZ plants thrive in medium to low indirect light.
    TIP: ZZ can tolerate bright indirect light.

    Water
    You should water every 2-3 weeks.
    Allow soil to dry out between.

    Fertilizer
    For optimal growth, ZZ plants should be fertilized every few months.
    Typically, zz plants should be fertilized during growing season (Spring).
